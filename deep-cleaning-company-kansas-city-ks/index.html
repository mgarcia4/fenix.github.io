WHAT DO I DO WITH MY PETS WHEN YOU ARE CLEANING?

When you hire a deep cleaning service in Kansas City, KS, you might wonder what to do with your pets during the cleaning process. Here are some tips to ensure a stress-free experience for both you and your furry friends:

Plan Ahead: Before the cleaning appointment, make arrangements to keep your pets in a safe and comfortable space away from the cleaning area. This could be a separate room, a friend's house, or a pet daycare facility.

Inform the Cleaners: Let the cleaners know about your pets and any specific instructions or precautions they need to take. This will help them plan their cleaning process accordingly and avoid any disruptions.

Secure Pets: If you prefer to keep your pets at home during the cleaning, make sure they are secure in a crate or a designated area. This will prevent them from getting underfoot or causing any accidents during the cleaning.

Provide Comfort: Keep your pets comfortable with their favorite toys, blankets, and treats in their designated area. This will help reduce their anxiety and make the cleaning process smoother for everyone involved.

Follow Up: After the cleaning is done, check on your pets to ensure they are calm and relaxed. Spend some quality time with them to reassure them that everything is back to normal.
