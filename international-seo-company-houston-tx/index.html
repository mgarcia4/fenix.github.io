Structural Changes:

  • Optimizing your website's structure for international SEO involves implementing hreflang tags to indicate language and regional targeting. This helps search engines understand which version of your content to serve to users based on their location and language preferences.

Structuring your website in a way that caters to the preferences of your target audience in different countries can boost your international SEO performance.
