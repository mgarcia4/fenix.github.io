WHAT DO I DO WITH MY PETS WHEN YOU ARE CLEANING?

When it comes to getting your carpets professionally cleaned in Blue Springs, MO, you may be wondering what to do with your beloved pets during the process. We understand that your furry friends are an important part of your family, so here are some tips to ensure a smooth carpet cleaning experience:

1. Keep them in a safe space: During the carpet cleaning service, it's best to keep your pets in a separate room or area of the house where they will be comfortable and safe. This will prevent any accidents or disruptions during the cleaning process.

2. Inform the cleaners: Let the professional cleaners know in advance if you have pets in the house. This way, they can take necessary precautions and work around your pet's needs to ensure a thorough and effective cleaning job.

3. Schedule accordingly: If possible, try to schedule the carpet cleaning service at a time when your pets can be taken out for a walk or kept in a pet daycare for a few hours. This will make the process more convenient for both you and your pets.

4. Monitor their behavior: While the cleaning is underway, keep an eye on your pets to ensure they are safe and comfortable. If needed, provide them with their favorite toys or treats to keep them occupied.

5. Post-cleaning precautions: After the carpet cleaning is complete, make sure to keep your pets off the freshly cleaned carpets for a certain period as advised by the cleaners. This will help maintain the cleanliness and integrity of the carpets.
