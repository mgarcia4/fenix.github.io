  • On-page SEO refers to the optimization strategies you implement on your website itself to improve search engine rankings.
  • It involves optimizing elements such as meta tags, headers, content, and images for relevant keywords related to your Peoria business.
  • By focusing on on-page SEO, you can enhance the user experience, increase website traffic, and improve your site's visibility in local search results.

Off-Page SEO:

  • Off-page SEO helps increase your website's relevance and trustworthiness in the eyes of search engines, ultimately boosting your rankings and attracting more potential customers.
