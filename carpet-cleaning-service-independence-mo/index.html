WHAT DO I DO WITH MY PETS WHEN YOU ARE CLEANING?

When you schedule a professional carpet cleaning service in Independence, MO, there may be some considerations to keep in mind when it comes to your furry friends. Here are a few tips to ensure a smooth cleaning process while keeping your pets safe and comfortable:

Plan Ahead: Before the cleaning appointment, make arrangements for your pets to be in a secure and comfortable area of your home. This could be a room where they can relax without being disturbed or a crate if needed.

Inform the Technicians: Let the cleaning technicians know about your pets ahead of time so they can take any necessary precautions. This will help them work efficiently while ensuring the safety of your pets.

Keep Pets Away: During the cleaning process, it's best to keep your pets away from the area being cleaned. This will prevent any potential accidents or interference with the cleaning equipment.

Post-Cleaning Caution: After the carpet cleaning is complete, allow some time for the carpets to dry thoroughly before letting your pets back into the cleaned areas. This will help maintain the cleanliness of your carpets and prevent any tracking of dirt or moisture.
